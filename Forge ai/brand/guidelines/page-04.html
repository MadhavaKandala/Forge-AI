THE FORGE
V1 — BRAND GUIDELINES

"Heat doesn't ask permission. Neither do you." — every line of copy should sound like it could follow that sentence.

01 — Direct

Say what happened. No hedging, no qualifiers, no "maybe consider."

✓  "47-day streak. Longest yet."
✕  "Wow, you're crushing it! 47 days and counting!! 🔥🔥"

02 — Unsentimental

Facts over feelings. A missed day is a missed day, stated plainly.

✓  "Missed yesterday. Streak reset to 0."
✕  "Don't worry, everyone has off days! Tomorrow's a new start :)"

03 — Earned

Praise is reserved for real thresholds — PRs, milestones, completed programs. Never for showing up.

✓  "Personal best: 82.5kg squat."
✕  "Great job logging your workout today!"

04 — Quiet Intensity

Confidence without volume. State the milestone and let it sit — no exclamation points, no emoji walls.

✓  "Forged. Day 100."
✕  "🏆 INCREDIBLE MILESTONE UNLOCKED! 🏆"
