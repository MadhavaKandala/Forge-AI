Brand Pillars
Three forces, one shape
THE FORGE
V1 — BRAND GUIDELINES
01 Heat

The energy that starts the work. Urgency without panic — today's session matters because today is the only day you can act in.

In the product
  • Today's session card lit in lime, everything else dimmed
  • Streak flame / heat indicator on the home screen
  • Single accent color per screen — never competing highlights
02 Pressure

What accumulates. Every logged day adds weight to the next — the stakes of breaking a streak are real and visible, not hidden behind soft language.

In the product
  • Streak counter as a primary, large numeral — Day 47, not "47 days! 🎉"
  • Program week progress bars that fill, not refresh
  • Resets shown plainly — no apology copy
03 Repetition

The daily return. Same screen, same rhythm, same single light — consistency is the design, not an add-on feature.

In the product
  • Calendar / streak grid as a recurring visual motif
  • Daily mood check-in as a fixed ritual, same position every day
  • Recurring program structure — weeks repeat, intensity builds

How they combine

Heat starts a session. Pressure is what's riding on it. Repetition is what turns one session into a shape that holds. Every screen in Forge AI should be answerable to at least one of these three — if a feature serves none of them, it doesn't belong.
