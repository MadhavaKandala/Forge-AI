Black dominates every screen. Lime is reserved for the single most important element per view — never two lime elements competing for attention. Orange marks secondary signals (labels, tags, world-building copy). Bone White carries body text only.

Pairing Rules

  • Lime never sits on Orange, or vice versa — both read as "primary"
  • Bone White text on Black only — 13.6:1 contrast ratio
  • Black text on Lime or Bone for inverted surfaces

Don't

  • Don't introduce new accent hues without updating this page
  • Don't use pure black (#000) or pure white (#FFF)
  • Don't tint Black toward blue/warm — keep it neutral
